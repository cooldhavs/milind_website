About Audi R8 and Video




For the past 16 years, Audi has been a dominating force at the most prestigious endurance race in Europe – 24 Hours of Le Mans. One of the innovations that pushed Audi into the winner’s circle was the premiere of laser lights. During the late-night hours of Le Mans, laser lights gave the Audi team drivers the added advantage of being able to see more with a light that was further reaching and more intense.

This advanced lighting upgrade comes standard in the 2017 Audi R8 V10 plus Coupé.
